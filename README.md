<h1>Jetpack Joyride</h1>

<h4>Description:</h4>
<p>Jetpack Joyride is an endless runner game where players take control of Barry Steakfries, a jetpack-loving adventurer who must escape a secret laboratory filled with zappers, missiles, lasers, and other crazy contraptions. The goal is to fly as far as possible, dodging obstacles and collecting coins along the way.</p>

<h4>Basic Requirements:</h4>
<p><h5>1. Endless Runner:</h5> The game should be an endless runner, meaning the level procedurally generated and the player continuously moves forward.</p>
<p><h5>2. Jetpack Control:</h5> Players should control Barry's jetpack with a simple one-touch system. Tapping or holding the space key makes him rise, and releasing makes him fall.</p>
<p><h5>3. Obstacle Variety:</h5> There should be a variety of obstacles to dodge, including zappers, missiles, lasers, robotic arms, and even giant mechanical dragons, and introduce occasional bosses requiring the player to use their skills and collected power-ups to defeat them.</p>
<p><h5>4.Coin Collection:</h5> Players should collect coins scattered throughout the level to earn points.</p>
<p><h5>5.Power-ups:</h5> Random power-ups should appear throughout the level, granting temporary abilities like invincibility, coin magnets, or score multipliers.</p>
<p><h5>6.Humorous Tone:</h5> Jetpack Joyride should maintain a lighthearted and humorous tone, with silly characters, animations, and sound effects.</p>

<h4>Extra Features:</h4>
<p><h5>1. Missions and Achievements:</h5> The game should include a variety of missions and achievements to give players long-term goals and encourage replayability.</p>
<p><h5>2. Costume System:</h5> The game should feature a variety of costumes for Barry to wear.</p>
<p><h5>3.Jetpack Upgrades:</h5> Players should be able to use coins to upgrade their jetpack, increasing its power, fuel capacity, or firing rate.</p>
